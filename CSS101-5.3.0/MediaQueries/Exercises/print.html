Classic Scrambled Eggs

Classic scrambled eggs are the best scrambled eggs!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Red Peppers
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Crack eggs into bowl.
  2. Add milk and beat until mixed smoothly.
  3. Put the pan to medium heat. Coat pan with a thin layer of butter.
  4. Fry the red peppers and onions until they look ready, and then pour the eggs on top.
  5. Sprinkle a bit of salt over everything.
  6. With spatula or fork stir the eggs consistently from the middle.
  7. When the eggs looks ready, turn off the heat and throw some cheese, pepper and salt on top of the eggs.
  8. Serve and you are done!
